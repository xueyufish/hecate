## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Analyze success trajectories
The system SHALL analyze successful conversation trajectories to extract best practices.

#### Scenario: Extract success patterns
- **WHEN** a conversation ends successfully
- **THEN** the system analyzes the trajectory and extracts key success factors

### Requirement: Analyze failure trajectories
The system SHALL analyze failed conversation trajectories to extract improvement points.

#### Scenario: Extract failure patterns
- **WHEN** a conversation ends with failure
- **THEN** the system analyzes the trajectory and identifies improvement opportunities
